Who We Are

Guna's Lathe Works & Machining is a leading precision machining and metal fabrication workshop located in Thingalur, Erode, Tamil Nadu. Founded by Mr. Gunasekar, the company has grown over 15+ years into a trusted name for high-quality lathe components, welding, and custom fabrication.

We serve industries across Erode, Coimbatore, Tiruppur, and the surrounding regions — from small workshops to large industrial factories. Our commitment to precision, quality, and customer satisfaction drives everything we do.

OUR MISSION

To deliver exceptional machining and fabrication services that exceed customer expectations through precision craftsmanship, innovative solutions, and unwavering commitment to quality. We strive to be the go-to partner for all metalworking needs — from simple lathe components to complex custom manufactured parts.

OUR SERVICES

Lathe Machining

CNC and manual lathe turning, facing, threading, boring, and grooving. Tolerances up to ±0.01mm. Diameter range: 10mm–300mm, Length: up to 1500mm.

Welding Services

Professional MIG, TIG, and Arc welding. Materials: MS, SS, Aluminium, Cast Iron. Certified welders with visual and PT inspection on all joints.

Custom Fabrication

End-to-end fabrication from design to delivery. Industrial structures, conveyor frames, machine guards, and any custom metal components per your drawings.
